### Permissions
SDK must require Location permissions enabled including background location permission to collect fused location data that helps our algorithms provide efficient results.
You can refer to Android developers guidelines to integrate location permissions and user consent. Additional permission to access local storage is required and permission to check availablility of internet and wifi state.
