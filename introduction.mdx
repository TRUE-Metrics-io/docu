## Introduction
Welcome to **truemetrics Quickstart**! This guide is designed to help you integrate truemetrics into your system quickly and seamlessly. Whether you're a first-time user or revisiting for a refresher, you'll find actionable steps and examples to simplify your integration process.

This documentation serves two purposes:  
1. A hands-on guide to integrating our technology.  
2. A resource enriched with examples to illustrate input/output formats. These examples are intended as guidance, providing a clear path while leaving room for customization.

## SDK-Enabled Features

The **truemetrics Android SDK** enables you to collect precise positional and motion data from devices. Using this data, our technology identifies where couriers park and how they access buildings. This empowers couriers to optimize the last 50 meters of the delivery journey.

Our core features fall into three categories:

truemetrics Android SDK provides an easy way to collect the positional & motion data of the devices and convert them into intelligent insights to solve many challenges in the last mile delivery likes finding correct entrances and parking places but also getting deep delivery insights.
