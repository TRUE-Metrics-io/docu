## Introduction
Welcome to truemetrics Quickstart! This guide will help you get up and running with truemetrics in no time. Whether you're a first-time user or looking for a refresher, we've streamlined the process to ensure you can start leveraging the power of your data effortlessly. Follow these steps to unlock actionable insights today!


## SDK enabled features

truemetrics Android SDK provides an easy way to collect the positional & motion data of the devices and convert them into intelligent insights to solve many challenges in the last mile delivery likes finding correct entrances and parking places but also getting deep delivery insights.
